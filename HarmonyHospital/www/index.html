Experienced and Excellence in Healthcare. Providing state-of-the-art medical care for our patients. Description

The team of developers comprising Pratham Patel, Neel Savani, Nitya Patel, and Romnick Zinampan collaborated to create a comprehensive hospital management application using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and jQuery Mobile. Their combined efforts resulted in a functional and efficient application tailored to streamline hospital operations, including patient management, appointment scheduling, and a user-friendly interface for both staff and patients.
